About SASSA Grants

The South African Social Security Agency (SASSA) provides social grants to support vulnerable individuals and families who require government assistance.

These grants play a vital role in reducing poverty and providing basic services to those in need across South Africa.

  • Social Relief of Distress (SRD) R350 grant
  • Child Support Grant (CSG)
  • Older Persons Grant
  • Disability Grant
